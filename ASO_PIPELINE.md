## 4. Risk & Compliance Checklist
- [ ] **No Deceptive Claims**: Ensure we don't claim to "guarantee" results or "bypass" AI limits.
- [ ] **Permission Justification**: Every permission in `manifest.json` must have a clear, user-facing benefit.
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: Must explicitly state that no user content is stored or persisted.
- [ ] **No Keyword Stuffing**: Titles and descriptions must be human-readable and natural.
